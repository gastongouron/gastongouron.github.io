Editing my logs every weeks for two months
2009

While using my keyboard, a text editor automatically opens up and keeps track of all the keys being pressed. Every pressed-key is registered, saved as precise logs. Finally logs are edited every week for two monthes.
A basic font is created to hide my passwords and very personnal informations.
